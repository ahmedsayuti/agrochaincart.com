Platform Features

Everything a food system
needs, in one place.

The AgroChain Cart webapp is a full-stack agricultural marketplace — built for every actor in the supply chain, from the smallest family farm to the largest institutional buyer.

Live Produce Marketplace

Browse 20+ categories of fresh, regional produce from verified farms across California, New York, Texas, Florida, Washington and more — with real-time inventory, harvest dates, and farmgate pricing visible to every buyer.

Wishlist & Smart Cart

How It Works

From field to fork
in four steps.

The platform orchestrates the entire supply chain — no phone calls, no paperwork, no middlemen.

01

Farmer Lists Produce

Verified growers add new listings in under 60 seconds — setting price, unit, quantity, organic status, and region. Listings go live on the marketplace immediately.

02

Buyer Discovers & Orders

Households, restaurants, and institutions filter produce by region, category, and certification. AI demand signals surface what's trending so buyers always find what they need.

03

Platform Coordinates Logistics

AgroChain routes the order through its cold-chain partner network automatically. Farmers get a pick-up notification; buyers get live tracking. No third-party calls needed.

04

Faster Payouts, Fresh Food

Farmers receive payouts within 2 business days of order confirmation. Buyers receive produce within 48 hours of harvest — fresher than any supermarket supply chain can match.
