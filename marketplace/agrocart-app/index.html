🌾 Farmers Market Promotion Program (FMPP)

Federal grants supporting local and regional food systems. Funds direct producer-to-consumer markets, regional food hubs, and agri-tourism operations. Eligible entities include agricultural businesses, non-profits, and producer networks.

Up to $500,000
Applications Open Deadline: March 15

🏪 Local Food Promotion Program (LFPP)

Supports development of local and regional food business enterprises that process, distribute, aggregate, or store locally-produced food. Ideal for food hubs, cooperatives, and farm networks looking to expand distribution capacity.

Up to $500,000
Applications Open Deadline: March 15

🌱 Organic Certification Cost Share

USDA reimburses up to 75% of organic certification costs (max $500/year) for certified organic producers and handlers. Rolling applications accepted year-round through your State Department of Agriculture.

Up to $500 / year
Rolling applications

🚛 Value-Added Producer Grant (VAPG)

For independent producers or producer groups wanting to create value-added products. Covers planning activities, working capital, and marketing expenses for new value-added ventures.

Up to $75,000 (planning) · $250,000 (working capital)
Opens Q2 2026
